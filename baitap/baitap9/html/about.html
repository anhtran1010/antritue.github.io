About Al Fresco's

Our story

Based on true Aussie hospitality, Al Fresco’s serves some of the tastiest Western favorites in both Hanoi and Saigon. Offering a mix of Tex-Mex, Italian, and Australian food complemented with cold local imported beers and a wine list, featuring Australian's finest &affordable South American reds, Al Fresco's is the best choice for a big feed at a great price.

Considered a “godsend” by the expat community and very popular with today’s younger Vietnamese generation, with it’s casual & buzzy vibe, checkered table cloths, golden oldies playlist and friendly warm service, Al Fresco’s offers a welcoming oasis in the city’s hustle & bustle.

Famous for our fall-off-the-bone tender BBQ ribs & larger-than-life portions, the one thing you should definitely not forget at home is your appetite. Offering delivery as well as take away, Al Fresco’s is the best choice for a slice of home.

Locations

Today, Al Fresco's has twenty restaurants, 13 in Ha Noi, 2 in Hoi An - Danang and 7 in Ho Chi Minh City.
